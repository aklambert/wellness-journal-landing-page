# Journal
Use the Journal to journal about anything you'd like, or write notes.

Access this feature by selecting the **Journal** bottom navigation icon, then **Journal**

   <img src={journalFeaturesImg} style={{width: 240}} />

## Save a new journal entry
To save a new journal entry, follow these steps: 

1. Click the **+** button in the bottom right corner of the screen on the **Journal** screen: 

    <img src={newJournalEntryBtn} style={{width: 240}} />

2. You will then be taken to the **Add Journal Entry** screen, where you can enter a journal entry with whatever content you’d like to write 
3. When you’re ready to save your journal entry, click the **SAVE** button: 
 
    <img src={saveJournal} style={{width: 200}} />

4. Your journal entry will then be saved. You'll be redirected to the **Journal** screen, where you'll see your new journal entry in the list of entries

## Edit a journal entry
To edit a saved journal entry, follow these steps: 

1. Navigate to the **Journal** page, where all your saved journal entries are listed 
2. Click on the journal entry you'd like to edit 
 
    <img src={selectJournalEntry} style={{width: 240}} />

3. You will be taken to the **Update Journal Entry** screen, where you can edit the journal entry
4. When you’re ready to save changes, click the **SAVE** button: 
 
    <img src={updateJournalEntry} style={{width: 200}} />
 
5. Your changes will then be saved, and you will then be taken back to the **Journal** screen 

## Delete a journal entry
To delete a saved journal entry, follow these steps: 

1. Navigate to the **Update Journal Entry** screen for the journal entry you would like to delete. 
    You can see how to navigate to this screen [here](./journal#edit-a-journal-entry)
2. Click the **DELETE** button: 

       <img src={deleteJournalEntry} style={{width: 200}} />
    
3. The journal entry will then be deleted, and you will be redirected back to the **Journal** screen
