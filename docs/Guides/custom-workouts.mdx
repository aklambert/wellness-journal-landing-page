# Custom Workouts

Build custom workouts using exercises you've added.

Access **Workout** features by selecting the **Fitness** bottom navigation icon, then selecting **Workouts**

   <img src={fitnessFeaturesImg} style={{width: 240}} />

## Save a new workout
To save a new workout, follow the following steps: 

1. Click the **+** button in the bottom right corner of the screen on the Workouts screen: 
 
    <img src={addWorkoutScreen} style={{width: 240}} />

2. You will then be taken to the **Add Workout** screen, where you can enter workout information for your new custom workout. 
    You can add as much or as little information as you’d like. 
    There will also be a checklist of all your saved exercises, where you can select any exercise to add to your new workout  
3. Once you’re finished entering information for your workout, click **Save** at the bottom of the screen: 

    <img src={saveWorkout} style={{width: 200}} />

4. Your new workout will now be saved, and you will be taken back to the **Workouts** Screen

## Edit a workout
To edit a saved workout, follow these steps: 

1. Navigate to the **Workouts** page, where all your saved workouts are listed 
2. Click on the workout you would like to edit:
 
        <img src={selectWorkout} style={{width: 240}} />

3. You will be taken to the **Update Workout** screen, where you can adjust any of the information you would like to
4. When you’re ready to save changes, click the **Save** button at the bottom of the screen: 
 
   <img src={saveWorkoutEdit} style={{width: 200}} /> 

5. Your changes will then be saved, and you will then be taken back to the **Workouts** screen

## Delete a workout
To delete a saved workout, follow these steps: 

1. Navigate to the **Update Workout** screen for the exercise you would like to delete (see steps above for editing a workout) 
2. Click the **Delete** button near the bottom of the screen: 
 
   <img src={deleteWorkout} style={{width: 200}} /> 
    
3. The workout will then be deleted, and you will be redirected back to the **Workouts** screen

    :::warning Note  

        The last saved workout currently cannot be deleted to keep the app working correctly. 
        It can, however, still have all of its information adjusted. 
        If you try to delete the most recently added workout, there will be a message displayed, and the workout will not be deleted

    :::

   <img src={cannotDeleteWorkout} style={{width: 240}} /> 

## View and progress through a workout
To view a workout to complete, follow these steps: 

1. Navigate to the **Workouts** page, where all your saved workouts are listed 
2. Click the play icon on the workout you’d like to view and/ or complete: 

       <img src={workoutProgressionBtn} style={{width: 200}} />  
 
3. You will then be brought to a screen that shows the workout details including a checklist of exercises in that workout, and a timer. 
    You can check off exercises as you complete them to keep track of your workout progress. 
    
    <img src={completeWorkout} style={{width: 180}} />
